## Decision

R1 produces usable grouped rankings and improves walk-forward NDCG over the deterministic and classifier baselines, but the selected top-10 stream loses cost-adjusted excess return in both validation scopes. It therefore fails the economic floor and must not be promoted, served, or treated as a trade recommendation. R2 and O1 remain separate ablations; neither may reuse this rejected artifact as a production base.
